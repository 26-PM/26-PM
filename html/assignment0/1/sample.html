India

Land of Diversity

India is a country located in South Asia. It is the seventh-largest country in the world and the second-most populous country, with over 1.3 billion people.

India is known for its diverse culture, beautiful landscapes, and ancient history. It is home to a variety of languages, religions, and traditions, making it a melting pot of cultures.

Attractions

  • Taj Mahal
  • Golden Temple
  • Qutub Minar

Cuisine

Indian cuisine is known for its rich and flavorful dishes, using a variety of spices and herbs. Some popular dishes include tandoori chicken, biryani, and samosas.
